# literature/survey/

Surveys synthesised from actually reading the papers in `../papers/`. Expected to grow and
be revised over many sessions.

## Files
- **[MLConcepts.md](MLConcepts.md)** — standing primer on ML vocabulary for a mathematician /
  systems engineer who is not an ML specialist. Append new concepts here rather than
  re-explaining them inline in surveys.
- **[01-landscape.md](01-landscape.md)** — high-level map of the 22 archived papers: six
  methodological groups, the shared mathematical substrate, where the math converges vs.
  fundamentally diverges, and an agenda of candidate mathematical blind spots.

## Conventions
- Numbered surveys (`NN-topic.md`) are sequential outputs; later ones may revise earlier ones
  (note revisions explicitly rather than silently editing history).
- ML jargon is offloaded to `MLConcepts.md` and *italicised* on first use.
- Paper references link into `../papers/` and use the file stem as the short name.
- Math is written as LaTeX source.
